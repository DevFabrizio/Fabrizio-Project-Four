Art is transforming thoughts into the tangible

Matinicus was born from an idea of innovative creating, in its most radical form

Matinicus is the representation of the ideal restaurant. It encompasses all the aspects what we thought must be a dining experience. Our research explored the vast panaorama of what was possible. The result? All we needed was right here on our beloved island. From the resources to grow the our prime ingredients to the expertise and avant-garde proclivity of our kitchen staff. Be our guest and let us redifine your concept of "restaurant". Welcome to Matinicus.

Matinicus pledged to only select the freshest local ingredients. Each recipe is designed to enhance the seasonal flavors the island has to offer. Our commitement to reduce our carbon footprint starts right here. In our menu.

Lobster croutons with summer herbs aioli

Fresh caught Maine lobster, cooked in a vacuum seal to preserve its natural complexities, accompanied by a perfumed aioli scented by the native species of aromatic plants

Smoked salmon with blueberry balsamic reduction glaze

This wild salmon is slowly smoked using a pine wood variety which can only be found on this island, summer blueberris are hand-picked and reduced to a sweet and tangy glaze.

Strawberry Plum

A soft and delicate baked plum which includes honey from the wild flowers of the coastline. In addition it is filled with a strawberry puree obtained by a very rare and small variety of strawberries that grow on the island.

These are an excerpt from our summer menu. Reserve your table to get the full Matinicus experience.

Thomas Smith, founder and lead chef of Matinicus

Thomas started his career right here, on the Matinicus island and after a couple of years where he got his fundamentals dialed in, he decided to leave to explore his possibilities all around the world. After gaining an enviable amount of knowledge and expertise in multiple Michelin starred kitchens around the world he decided that it was time to come home. This is when he started this journey with the goal of re-discovery and improvement. Since then, everyday has been aimed towards the education of the diners utilizing the vast resources this island has to offer.
